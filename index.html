What is F.I.T.?
  • Personalized online workout program for special needs users
  • Initial screening surveys to develop best-fit workouts
  • Workout on you own time following pre-recorded videos
  • Videos tailor tofunctional mobility (balance, stamina coordination)
  • Accounts tracks progress of calories burned, steps taken, & videos completed!
